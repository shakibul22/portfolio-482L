Why I Loved Santorini

Santorini is one of those places that looks unreal in photos and somehow even better in person. The white houses tumbling down the cliffs, the blue church domes and the deep blue sea below made every corner feel like a postcard.

I spent evenings in Oia just waiting for the sun to go down, coffee in hand, watching the whole town glow orange.

  • Sunset in Oia
  • Walking the caldera path from Fira
  • The red and black sand beaches
  • Fresh seafood by the harbour

Best Time to Visit

Late spring and early autumn are ideal — warm enough to swim but without the peak-summer crowds and prices.

July and August are gorgeous but very busy, so if you can, aim for May, June or September for a more relaxed trip.
