Bali, Indonesia

The Island of the Gods

Why I Loved Bali

Bali was the first trip that really made me fall in love with travel. The mix of lush green rice terraces, friendly people and a slow, calm pace of life made it hard to leave.

Mornings started with fresh fruit and coffee, and most days ended watching the sunset from a quiet beach.

  • Tegallalang Rice Terraces
  • Uluwatu Temple at sunset
  • Snorkelling in Nusa Penida
  • The waterfalls near Ubud

Best Time to Visit

The dry season from April to October is the sweet spot — warm days, clear skies and calm seas that are perfect for diving.

If you like things quieter and cheaper, the shoulder months of April and October give you great weather without the big crowds.
